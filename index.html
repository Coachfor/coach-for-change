Mission

To provide mentorship and father figures through sports for young people in underserved communities.

Vision

A future where every child has someone to believe in them and guide them towards their full potential.

About Us

Coach for Change is an NPO that works with schools, coaches, and local teams to provide fatherhood mentorship. We are based in Mdantsane and aim to expand across the Eastern Cape.
